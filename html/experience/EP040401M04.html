Creating an N Community

You can create an N Community and join other Members on missions and win special prizes.

Creating an N-Community

1) Only one N Community can be created per customer. If you create an N Community, you automatically become the Master of that N Community.

2) Once you have created an N community, you cannot join another community.

3) If you are already a Member of another N Community, you cannot create a community.

4) When creating a community, membership applications are processed either immediately or upon approval by an N Community Master.

Making changes to the N Community

1) The community icon, community link, and the membership application method can be changed.

N Community Missions/Points/Prizes

1) When you create an N Community, you become the Master of the N Community. You can also participate in the N Community missions.

2) You earn Community Points when you participate in N Community missions.

3) The points you earn can be redeemed for prizes.

4) Only an N Community Master can apply to redeem prizes.

5) Community prizes awarded are allocated within the community, and the N Brand Service is not involved in the allocation process.

N Community Membership Approval

1) Depending on the N Community, membership applications are processed upon approval by the N Community Master.
