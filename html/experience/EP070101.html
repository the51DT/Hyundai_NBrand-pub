Hyundai Driving Experience

Add thrills to your skills

Speed Up Your Fun

Welcome to the world’s most iconic racetracks! Get ready for the ultimate driving experience with Hyundai as you improve your skills and lap-times second by second guided by professional instructors. Conquer legendary circuits like Germany’s Nürburgring, Valencia’s Circuit Ricardo Tormo in Spain, Italy’s Autodromo Nazionale Monza, and the TT Circuit in Assen, Netherlands. An exhilarating adventure awaits – on the track and in our exclusive hospitality package for you.

Ignite Your Driving Passion

In Hyundai Driving Experience, you can experience driving on the world's tarmac of iconic circuits. Prepare for ultimate driving adventures while you unleash your passion for speed and precision. The multi-level Hyundai Track Experience Driving Experience are your gateway to unparalleled excitement, top-tier training, and extraordinary hospitality.

Hyundai IONIQ 5 N:
Ready, When You Are

When innovation meets exhilaration, you’re in the driver seat of the Hyundai IONIQ 5 N.
With its state-of-the-art electric drivetrain delivering impressive torque and
exceptional acceleration it's right at the heart of Hyundai Driving Experience.
Equipped with a high-capacity battery, the Hyundai IONIQ 5 N ensures sustained performance on every lap – whether it takes place on world-famous circuits like the Nürburgring Nordschleife or anywhere else.
Designed to redefine your driving experience, the Hyundai IONIQ 5 N embodies next-generation performance.
