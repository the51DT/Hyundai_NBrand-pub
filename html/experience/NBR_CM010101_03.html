Hyundai N Lounge Jeonju

Skybox provided by Hyundai Motors N for customers using Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors FC.
It can be rented (for a fee) on the day of the game, and is a space where you can enjoy a perfect view.

Sky Box

It can be used for various events/meetings through rental (paid).
There are 3 screens and 2 private restrooms for comfortable viewing of the game.

The thrill of watching a game

Hyundai N Lounge Jeonju, located on the west side of Jeonju World Cup Stadium, is a private game viewing space that can accommodate up to 24 people.

Dynamically before and after the game

The highest-spec racing simulator and various N Collection merchandise are on display, allowing you to spend your waiting time before and after the race in a variety of ways (on-site purchase of merchandise is not possible).

Thrilling both indoors and outdoors

You can watch the game lively not only indoors, but also from the 24 independent outdoor bleachers outside the lounge.

Information Use

Notice

  • Use may inevitably be restricted due to unavoidable circumstances such as the spread of infectious diseases or bad weather.
  • Lounge use may not be available depending on Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors FC operating circumstances.

Hyundai N Lounge Jeonju Reservation Information

  • On sports driving days, it can be rented for free only during open schedules, and is only available to Hyundai N vehicle customers.
  • The rental cost is 2 million won (accommodates up to 24 people in total, tickets included).
  • Inquiries about use and rental reservations can be made by calling the Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors FC Operations Office (063-273-1763).
