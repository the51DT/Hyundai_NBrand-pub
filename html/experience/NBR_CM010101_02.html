  • On sports driving days, it can be rented for free only during open schedules, and is only available to Hyundai N vehicle customers.
  • Inquiries about use of 'N Lounge and test lab rental reservations can only be made online on the Naver reservation/inquiry page through the ‘Go directly to test lab reservation’ button above.
