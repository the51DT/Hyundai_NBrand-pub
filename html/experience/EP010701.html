N Festival History

  • 2024

    Start of Electric Motorsports

    Launch of the first domestic one-make electric car race, eN1 Cup Addition of the Yongin Round.

  • 2023

    Splitting Sprint And Time Trial

    The final year for Veloster N and Avante N Line classes Hyundai Junior Driver.

    • 2022 season champion Junui Park competes in TCR Italy
    • 2022 season runner-up Junesung Park competes in 24h Nürburgring
  • 2022

    Beginning of Full-Fledged Professional Racing

    Launch of Avante N Cup car.

    Addition of Avante N Cup, Avante N Time Trial.

  • 2021

    Expanding Categories

    Veloster N Cup, Avante N Line Cup.

    Veloster N Time Trial, and Avante N Line Time Trial.

  • 2020

    Introducing Time Trial

    Addition of Veloster N Time Trial.

  • 2019

    Start of Hyundai N Festival

    Veloster N Cup, Avante Cup.
