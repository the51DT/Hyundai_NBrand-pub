i30 N

Shift the moment.

Overview

The highlight of the new i30 N

Introduction of the wet-type 8-speed dual clutch transmission with paddle shifters, increased power & torque for the Performance Pack, and lightweight forged alloy wheels.

Performance

Performance taken to the next level

The 2.0 T-GDI engine now delivers 280 PS and a substantial increase in flat power for more torque and
power at lower RPMs. This ensures high responsiveness, improved acceleration and much more fun.

Shift the moment.

The i30 N has been enhanced with a new design focused on performance, emotion and statement. It is also equipped with more lightweight materials, resulting in more agility and better handling.

Lightweight components for better performance.

The weight saved through the new high-performance N Light Seats and 19" forged alloy wheels results in better performance and
handling for a more agile driving experience.

The N 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission (N DCT)

A rightful addition on the new i30 N: the wet-type 8-speed dual clutch transmission with paddle shifters and new N performance functions for an even sportier experience.

The all-new N DCT

Available for the first time: a wet-type 8-speed dual clutch transmission with paddle shifters and new N performance functions for an even sportier experience.

Fast shift at your fingertips

The racing-inspired paddle shifters put fast, effortless gear changes at your fingertips. And they’re mounted on the steering wheel, you can shift more efficiently when cornering.

Design

An enhanced exterior design focused on dynamic performance

The new i30 N's design can be described in three words: performance, emotion, statement. Beyond an exercise in style, performance defines the changes made exclusively for the i30 N.

Rear spoiler and triangular brake light.

Striking new headlamp design.

The stylish new lighting design features integrated V-shaped Daytime Running Lights, as well as slimmer headlamps with the latest LED technology.

Designed with Sporty details

The new i30 N makes a powerful statement from every angle. On the side, glossy black door mirrors and body colour door handles harmonize with the N branded matte side skirt for a very sporty look.

Rear spoiler and triangular brake light.

For minimizing lift and maximizing fun. The rear spoiler with its integrated triangular brake light helps increase downforce to give you more grip through high-speed corners.

New rear bumper.

It’s a sporty and powerful look. The aggressively sculpted new rear bumper features a racing red character line and integrated fog lights above the new oversized exhaust pipes.

Bigger rear exhaust pipes

We’ve enlarged the dual single exhaust pipes to enhance the high-performance look of the new i30 N. Go big or go home!

Upgraded performance cockpit

Created for and by people who truly love cars, the new i30 N's sleek and sporty interior embraces and excites. The cockpit is as much about control as it is about comfort with the new N Light Seats and bigger touch screen.

N steering wheel.

Featuring exclusive Performance Blue stitching and the N logo, the leather N steering wheel puts driving performance features right at your fingertips – like for example, the dedicated buttons for N Mode and N Grin Shift.

N Cluster.

See engine oil temperature, torque, and turbo boost at a glance. The shift timing feature supports track driving. The red zone of the variable LED rev-counter changes according to the oil temperature.

N Light Seats- Sit like a pro.

Covered in premium leather and Alcantara® materials, the lightweight, high-performance monoform seats feature an illuminated N logo and pronounced bolsters for excellent lateral support.

N DCT shifter.

The N DCT shifter features the N logo, sporty metal accents, a Performance Blue racing line and stitching as well as leather inserts for better grip and even more motorsport feeling.
