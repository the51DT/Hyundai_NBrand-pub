N Battery Preconditioning (Drag Mode, Track Mode)

Pre-set the battery temperature for optimized performance

1. Before entering the circuit

2. Selecting the Drag mode optimizes the temperature (30-40°C) for the maximum output over a short period.

3. Selecting the Track mode optimizes the temperature (20-30°C) for continuous track driving.

Drag mode

Pre-conditions the battery for maximum power Target temperature is set at 30~40°C, ideal for fastest possible acceleration

Track mode

Pre-conditions the battery to account for steady temperature rise during track drive.

Target temperature is set at 20~30°C, allowing consistent performance for a number of lap

N Race (Sprint, Endurance)

Pre-sets the energy use strategy for improved resistance to power degradation on the track

1. Adapts to track conditions

2. For optimal racing performance

3. By managing energy consumption.

Sprint mode

Prioritizes immediate burst of full power Ideal for hot laps

Endurance mode

Prioritizes track range for long stints Ideal for non-competitive track driving
