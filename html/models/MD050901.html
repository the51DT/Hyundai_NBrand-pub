Performance

The new VELOSTER N

Now available with the N 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission, you can discover another side of the high-performance Corner Rascal. The new paddle shifters behind the steering wheel put power at your fingertips — so you can shift the moment anytime, anywhere.

The Corner Rascal

Hyundai's experience and knowledge of motorsports like WRC and TCR have led to the creation of Veloster N, the high-performance model made for the track and the road. It's nicknamed The Corner Rascal because it delivers incredible cornering ability in addition to its everyday sports car feel and race track capability.

The N 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

The N DCT is an automated manual transmission with two separate clutches for increased efficiency, faster shifting, and driving convenience. Its wet clutch system offers better cooling and copes with high torque, and comes with a simple gear lever arrangement so you can push forward to downshift and pull back to upshift. With high-performance logic enhancing the fun, you get full control over your drive.

N Power Shift

Power from the back for fun in the front seat. The N Power Shift (NPS) lets you engage with the car for the fun-to-drive feeling of a manual on the DCT.

N Grin Shift

N Grin Shift (NGS) gives you maximum performance at the press of a button. For 20 seconds you can enjoy a boost of performance like no other, utilizing the engine's overboost and N DCT's ideal shift pattern. Go on, you can't help but grin.

N Track Sense Shift

Veloster N senses how you're driving and automatically activates the N Track Sense Shift (NTS) once it knows you're racing. While on the track, the NTS shifts into the right gear for optimal performance — so you can focus on steering and enjoy the drive.
