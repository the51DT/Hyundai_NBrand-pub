N Vision 74

Overview

From Imagination into Reality

After seven years of envisioning hydrogen-powered high performance,
the N Vision 74 and sets Hyundai N's sights beyond electric cars.

Performance

Journey of Progress

N Vision 74 achieves perfect balance between state-of-the-art technology and iconic design. Long range and fast refueling characteristics of hydrogen fuel cells will change the way we enjoy driving. Even the way we race in motorsports.

1

Optimized Heat Management for Enhanced Race Track Capability

Individual cooling systems for each stack, battery, power electric module manage heat for N Vision 74’s enhanced race track capability.

2

Integrated Control System

Integrated control system manages optimal power distribution between fuel stack (max 95kW) mounted in the front and T-typed battery (62kWh) at the bottom making the best synergy with two different power source.

3

Torque Vectoring Control Unit

N Vision 74 is equipped with the Torque Vectoring logic (TVCU) for precise handling & dependable traction, resulting in accessible and enjoyable driving experience with two powerful motors in the rear that generate over 500kW (Rear 2EA).

4

Hydrogen Fuel Tank

Two hydrogen fuel tanks (4.2kg) on the rear axle and the second-generation fuel cell stack on the front axle offers a new motorsports opportunity with fast refueling and long driving range (600km ↑).

Keeping the Promise

Powered by hydrogen, Hyundai N 2025 Vision Gran Turismo was our dream for the future. Making dreams come true. That’s what we are doing at N.

Start From Scratch

Starting with structuring a new layout to place the FCEV system, our Ngineers decided to kick off the project with RWD assisted with twin motors. Moreover, N Vision 74 tested a unique logic with e-TVTM (electronic Torque Vectoring by Twin Motors) to enhance cornering experience with 235kW motors on each rear wheel.

Finding Optimal Balance

N Vision 74 had a mission to find the optimal balance between cooling and performance to withstand its inevitably heavier weight. With the 3 channel cooling system, heat management of the PE system is enhanced. Meanwhile, motorsports-inspired details led to better aerodynamics with elements such as the end-shape of the side skirt and the rear spoiler.

Future Opportunity in Motorsports

N Vision 74 is capable of driving over 600km and if can be refueled up to 80% SOF in just 5 minutes. Fast refueling characteristic also opens up opportunities for the next generation of motorsports. The courage to pursue this mission and the ability to embrace imagination will open a new chapter of driving fun.

Design

Envisioning The Future
of N with Legacy

N Vision 74 represents Hyundai design’s vision for the sustainable future.
Its design exceeds all expectations of what a future high performance car should look like.

Timeless Design

Inspired by Pony Coupe concept, N Vision 74 elevates Pony as Hyundai's timeless icon. Its design profile transcends time inspired from the past, while moving towards the future of high performance.

Functional Aesthetics

As N Vision 74 is the first high performance Rolling Lab built on the most advanced hydrogen fuel cell, the vehicle is designed to balance aerodynamics and cooling. With such functional aesthetics, the high performance technology is fully integrated into the design to fulfill the FCEV’s heat management requirements.

Entertaining Space

N Vision 74’s interior inherits the driver-centric cockpit of the Hyundai Pony Coupe concept. It blends heritage elements, modern design, and functional motorsport seating to create a unique interior environment. Performance Blue details have been added to create an N-specific interior feel.

From Imagination into Reality

Hyundai Pony Coupe 1974

Design & Heritage

N Vision 74 pays an homage to the Hyundai Pony Coupe concept from 1974, which was developed by the legendary car designer Giorgetto Giugiaro. The concept was then built into prototypes for Hyundai’s first production sports car. Although it could not reach production in the end, the daring attitude set the tone for the entire company. 50 years later, we are exceeding the wildest dreams with imagination and courage.

Hyundai N 2025 Vision GT

Brand & Tech

The Hyundai N 2025 Vision Gran Turismo was revealed at the 2015 Frankfurt Motor show with the launch of N brand. Meaning 'the vision of 2025', the name encapsulates that Hyundai will lead hydrogen technology into high performance. In just seven years, N Vision 74 experimented the driving and cooling performance of advanced FCEVs to realize the dream.

The Hyundai Pony Coupe Concept 01

Giorgetto Giugiaro of Italdesign designed and constructed the Pony Coupe concept for a small company called ‘Hyundai’.

The Hyundai Pony Coupe Concept 02

After its first public debut in 1974 Torino Motor show, Pony Coupe concept was brought back to Korea and was actively promoted to strengthen the Pony line-up.

The Hyundai Pony Coupe Concept 03

To initiate production, reverse engineering took place at Ulsan plant.

The Hyundai Pony Coupe Concept 04

Although it did not lead to mass production, N Vision 74 inherited the daring spirit of Pony Coupe concept as the first hydrogen hybrid Rolling Lab.
