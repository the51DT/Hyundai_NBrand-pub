Design

'N Option'
the New i30 N Show Car

The i30 N Option show car gives a first glimpse of how Hyundai’s high-performance options could evolve. With numerous customization features available, the company aims to cater to the needs of driving enthusiasts who want to make their vehicles more unique and special. Once launched, the ‘N Option’ will be ‘The Option’ of the near future. The ‘N Option’ delivers a total of 25 different high-quality exterior and interior individualization options.

The exterior options include a carbon rear wing carrying the N emblem, a carbon hood with integrated air vents, thrilling quad mufflers, and the N grille surrounded by red accents. Another highlight of the ‘N Option’ style and performance are the wheels with casted lightweight 20-inch rims, fitted with high-performance semi-slick tires. The exterior of the i30 N ‘N Option’ is coated in a unique matt paint, which was specially created for the show car.

A First Glimpse of the N Evolution

Black carbon accentuates the interior of the N alongside the dashboard, around the door handles and air vents, as well as the bottom of the steering wheel, which is covered with high-quality Alcantara. The N offers high-quality Alcantara for the upper dashboard and the lightweight sport bucket seats that are characterized by ornate red longitudinal lines. Finally, the ultimate performance car style is completed with a dynamic gear shift design, sports pedals, and multiple customizable options for your N interior.
