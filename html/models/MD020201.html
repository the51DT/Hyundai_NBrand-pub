Overview

Electrifying
motorsport passion

The IONIQ 5 N eN1 Cup car will compete in a dedicated one-make eN1 race class to be newly installed in the Hyundai N Festival race season held in South Korea. This is also an exercise in development to advance electric motorsports.

Motorsports is where every N is proven in fierce competition. The same requirement applies for our first EV without compromise. If the IONIQ 5 N has nowhere to race, we will make a race series for it.

Watch More

A one-of-a-kind
one-make race car

At its core, the new racer features the production IONIQ 5 N’s PE system including the battery, motors, and electronics as standard from factory. The chassis, safety and aerodynamics are a result of motorsports-grade development suitable for competition.

The inaugural season of eN1 class racing joins the Hyundai N Festival 2024 season in Korea. Can electric cars make our heart race? Let the motorsport spirit decide.

Unchanging passion changes everything

In motorsport there is no straight path for success. There are twists and turns, heartbreaks and failures, awarded by vanquishing victory.

The IONIQ 5 N eN1 Cup car ventures into an unknown chapter of electric motorsports. We savor the challenge to forge our electric cars into motorsport proven products.

There is no straight path for electrification. You can lift and slow down, "scale back”. Just like in motorsports, there is no time to hesitate.
