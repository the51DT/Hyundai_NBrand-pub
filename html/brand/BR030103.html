N-spired by Motorsport

A highly competent road car is essential as a basis for a highly competitive race car. This is the focus of a close collaboration between Namyang R&D and Europe Technical Center with integrated technology-sharing that results in mass-produced cars that share the same technological platform for development with racecars, simultaneously growing both organizations together.
