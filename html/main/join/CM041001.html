Membership Withdrawal Information

Please check the instructions before applying for N Brand membership withdrawal.

  • Withdrawing from N Brand membership and withdrawing from the Hyundai Motor Company integrated account are separate.

    When you withdraw from N Brand membership, your Hyundai Motor Company integrated account will not be cancelled.
    If you wish to cancel your Hyundai Motor Company account, please use the Unified Account Membership Cancellation option.
    When you withdraw your Hyundai Motor Company account, you will also cancel your N Brand membership.

  • Once you have created and joined N Community, you cannot withdraw membership.

    If you are an N Community creator, approval for closure must be completed, and if you are a member, you must withdraw.

  • If you cancel your membership, the nickname you used cannot be reused or restored.

    When you cancel your account, your nickname cannot be reused or restored by you or anyone else, so please choose your nickname carefully.

  • After withdrawal, all service usage records will be deleted.

    All personal usage records such as Posts, Missions, Rewards, N Community, points, and notifications will be deleted, and deleted data will not be recovered.

    Usage records deleted

    Notice Delete notification history

  • Even after withdrawal, posts registered and participated in bulletin board services and Nfluencer services will remain.

    Posts made in Post, Missions, Rewards, N Community, 1:1 Inquiry, etc. are not automatically deleted upon withdrawal and remain as is.
    If there is a post that you would like to delete, please be sure to delete it before unsubscribing.
    After membership withdrawal, your member information is deleted and there is no way to verify your identity, so we cannot delete your posts arbitrarily.
