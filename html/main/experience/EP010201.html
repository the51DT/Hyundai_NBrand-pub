Plan your visit

By car

Set your destination to Everland SPEEDWAY or 310 Jeondaeli, Pogok-eup, Cheoin-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do

※ Arrival time may vary depending on traffic conditions.

By public transport

By bus

From Seoul: Take bus 5002, 5700, 1500-2 or 1113
From Incheon or Gyeonggi-do: Take bus 8862, 8839, 66, or 66-4 → Then, take the shuttle in front of the main gate of Everland

By subway

Take Bundang Line and get off at Giheung Station → Take EverLine and get off at the last stop, and take the shuttle bus (for free) to get to Everland → Take the shuttle bus on front gate
