Closing an N Community

When an N Community is closed, any points held within the community will be lost, and it will not be possible to recreate the community under the same name.

Closing an N Community

1) Only the N Community Master can close the community.

2) Community closure requires administrator approval, and notifications will be sent regarding the approval status.

3) If closure is approved, all Members of an N Community will be removed from the community, and a notification regarding the closure will be sent to all Members of the N Community.

4) Recreate an N Community under the name of the closed community is not possible.

5) All Members of an N Community will be forcibly removed from the community.

Withdrawing a community closure application

1) Closing a community requires administrator approval, and the community closure application can be withdrawn before the administrator approval.

2) How to withdraw a closure application

You may do so under My Page > N Community > Close Community.

Remaining N Community Points

1) When a community is closed, any N Community Points held within the community will also be deleted.
