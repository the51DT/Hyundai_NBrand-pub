N Community Closure

Please read the instructions for closing an N Community.

Closing an N Community

1) After the closure has been requested, the admin must approve it.

Canceling The Closure of An N Community

1. Cancellation instructions

1) You can cancel the closure request only while it is pending for the admin's approval.
2) If the closure request is already under review for approval, it cannot be canceled.

2. How to cancel

1) You can cancel a closure request in [My Page > N Community > Settings > Closure].

N Community Missions

1. Active missions

1) You can modify or delete missions if they are pending for approval.
2) You cannot modify or delete missions if they are already under review.
- If the missions you participate in are approved after the closure, the points will not be awarded.
- Approved participation posts may be published at the discretion of the admin.

N Community Rewards

1. Rewards pending for application

1) All rewards pending for application will be canceled regardless of their approval status.

2. Rewards with application complete

1) Rewards for which the application was completed before the closure approval will be awarded.

N Community Points

1. Points instructions

1) Your points will be expired immediately.

N Community Restoration

1. Restoration instructions

1) Once the admin in charge of the closure request approves the closure, it cannot be restored.
