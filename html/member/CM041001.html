Membership Withdrawal

Membership withdrawal instructions

Please read the instructions before requesting to withdraw your Hyundai N membership.

  • Withdrawing your Hyundai N membership does not affect the unified Hyundai Motor account.

    Even after withdrawing your Hyundai N membership, your unified Hyundai Motor account will remain valid.
    If you wish to terminate the unified Hyundai Motor account, delete the unified account.
    Terminating the unified Hyundai Motor account will also withdraw your Hyundai N membership.

  • If you have created or joined an N Community, you cannot withdraw your membership.

    If you have created your own N Community, you must get approval for its closure. If you are a member, you must leave the community.

  • If you choose to withdraw, your nickname cannot be reused or restored.

    Remember, once withdrawn, your nickname cannot be reused or restored by anyone including yourself.

  • All service usage history will be deleted upon withdrawal.

    All personalized usage records, including posts, missions, rewards, N Community, points, notifications, will be deleted. Once deleted, they cannot be restored.

  • Even after withdrawal, the posts you created or participated in on the bulletin board and Nfluencer services will remain.

    What you wrote in posts, missions, rewards, N Community, and 1-on-1 inquiry will not be automatically deleted upon withdrawal.
    If you have any posts or comments you want to delete, make sure to delete them before withdrawal.
    After withdrawal, your information will be deleted. So we cannot verify your identity nor delete your posts arbitrarily.
