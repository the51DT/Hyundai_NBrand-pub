N Community Transfer

N Community transfer request instructions

Please read the instructions for applying for transfer of N Community.

Your community needs to meet one of the following conditions to request a transfer.

1. Criminal act conducted by the master

2. The master is negligent in management or inactive for a long period

3. The master’s failure to transfer the master status before withdrawing the membership

For a transfer request, please fill out the request form below and contact us through the 1:1 inquiry.

1. Items needed for a transfer request

1) N Community Name

2) Reasons for the request

3) The nickname of a member to whom the N Community will be transferred

4) Email address where the transfer request result will be sent

2. How to cancel a transfer request

1) Write a reason for the cancellation request and contact us through the 1:1 inquiry.

Master status is transferred if the admin deems the transfer request reasonable.
