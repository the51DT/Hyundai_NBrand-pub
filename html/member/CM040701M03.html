Transfer information

N Community Transfer information

Please check the precautions when transferring N Community.

N Community transfer

1. Transfer information

1) Only the Master can be transferred.

2) It can only be transferred to the relevant N Community member.

3) Participating N Community – There must be no master mission.

4) There must be no rewards being applied for.

5) The consent of the member receiving the transfer is required.

6) Upon consent to transfer, it will be transferred immediately.

N Community transfer cancellation

1. Cancellation information

1) After requesting a transfer, cancellation is possible until the transfer is approved.

2) If the transfer request is rejected, it will be automatically canceled.

2. How to cancel transfer

1) This can be done through the My Page > N Community > Settings > Transfer menu.

N Community Point

1. Points information

Points held will be transferred to the member receiving the transfer.

N Community re-transfer

1. Re-transfer information

1) If you wish to transfer Master rights again, you must consult with the Master.

2) N Brand is not involved in transfer.

For additional inquiries, please use 1:1 Inquary.

Please select the member to whom you wish to transfer.
