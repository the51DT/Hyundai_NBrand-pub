Membership Withdrawal

Notification of membership withdrawal

Please check the instructions before applying for Hyundai N membership withdrawal.

  • The withdrawal of Hyundai N membership and the withdrawal of Hyundai Motor's integrated account are separate.

    Hyundai Motor's integrated account will not be withdrawn when members of N Brand leave.

  • If you leave the membership, the nickname you used cannot be reused or restored.

    When withdrawing, the nickname cannot be reused or restored for both you and others, so please choose carefully.

  • After withdrawal, all service usage records will be deleted.

    Personal usage records such as Post, Missions, Rewards, N Community, Points, and Notifications are all deleted, and deleted data is not recovered.

    Usage records deleted
    record detail
    Post Post, like, delete report history
    Missions Delete participation history and posts
    Rewards Delete application details and posts
    N Community Create/submit, participate in the preview, delete the reward application history, retention points, and posts
    Point Extinction of holding points
    Notice Delete notification history

  • Post registration and participation in bulletin board-type and Nfluencer services remain after withdrawal.

    Posts written on Post, Missions, Rewards, N Community, 1:1 inquiries, etc., are not automatically deleted upon withdrawal and remain the same.
    If there is a post you want to delete, please make sure to delete it before you leave.
    After withdrawing from the membership, the membership information is deleted, so there is no way to check whether it is you, so we cannot delete the post arbitrarily.

    Table of items maintained
    record detail
    Post Post, like, report history
    Missions Participation, reporting history and posts
    Rewards Application details and posts
    N Community Mission Participation Post

After you leave, you cannot rejoin with the nickname you are using and your data cannot be recovered.
Postings that remain in the Bulletin and Nfluencer services remain and cannot be deleted after withdrawal.
