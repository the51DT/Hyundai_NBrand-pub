Deleting your membership

Notification of
Deleting your membership

Please read the instructions before deleting your Hyundai N Membership.

  • Deleting your Hyundai N Membership will not delete your Hyundai Motor Account.

    Even if you delete your Hyundai N Membership, your Hyundai Motor Account will still remain active.
    If you wish to delete your Hyundai Motor Account, please use the Hyundai Motor Account deletion process.
    When you delete your Hyundai Motor Account, your Hyundai N Membership will be deleted as well.

  • Please note that once you delete your membership, the nickname associated with that membership cannot be reused or recovered.

    Please make your decision carefully before deleting your membership as your nickname will not be available for reuse or recovery by you or anyone else once deleted.

  • Once you delete your membership, all service usage records will be deleted.

    All personal usage records, such as posts, missions, N Community activities, points, and notifications, will be deleted, and the deleted data cannot be recovered.

  • Even after deleting your membership, the content you posted and participated in on the bulletin board service and the Nfluencer service will not be deleted.

    Your activities on these services, such as posts, missions, N Community activities, and 1:1 inquiries are not automatically deleted when you delete your membership.
    If you wish to delete any of those activities, please do so before deleting your membership.
    Once you delete you membership, those activities cannot be deleted as there is no means to verify your identity as the owner of those activities.
